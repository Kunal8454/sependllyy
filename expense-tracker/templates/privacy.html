Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (name, email) and the expense data you enter (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions). We also collect basic usage data such as page views and feature interactions to improve the App.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the expense tracking service. We do not sell your personal information. Expense data is used to generate your personal reports and insights within the App.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law. The App uses essential web technologies (cookies, local storage) only for session management and core functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. For any privacy-related concerns, please contact our support team.
